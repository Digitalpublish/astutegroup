Accessibility Services

Accessibility is the ability of a website, mobile app, or digital file to be readily navigated and interpreted by a wide range of users, including those who have visual, auditory, sensory and mental disabilities. Digital accessible products that follow universal design features easily adapt to the requirements or preferences of different users and can be accessed in more than one way. For example digital products like video streaming content as a best practices provides captions for hearing impaired people and websites that follow best practices include graphics/image alt text tags.

Our accessibility development process of PDF, PPT, HTML, ePub/eBook, MS-Word and MS-Excel files we follow below standards:

  • Compliance with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG)

  • Color contrast checking

  • Adding audio and video files with close-caption content

  • Handling images with alt text

  • Following proper content sequence

  • Following semantic tagging and structure

  • Appropriate linking format

  • Using MathML content with proper description

  • Alt text creation for image

  • Accessibility QA/testing for files for WCAG

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) Services

Data has the power to drive businesses to certain success. However, this data needs to be easily retrievable in a digital format for maximized value to be generated from it. In the case of scanned documents with printed, typewritten or handwritten text, retyping data from the document or its scan into a computer to make it editable is an extremely time-consuming task, which can distract your employees from focusing on core competencies. We offers Optical Character Recognition (OCR) services that can converted into editable data format from a scanned document, thereby improving your workflow and productivity. OCR is a technology through which various kinds of pictorial and textual data can be read, analyzed and organized into an electronic format. It is usually used in tandem with Intelligent Character Recognition (ICR) and Magnetic Ink Character Recognition (MICR), and plays an important role in data management of scanned documents. We take care of all of your OCR scanning and data conversion needs. Using the latest technology that optimizes accuracy and time ensuring that an immense number of documents can be converted speedily. The technology is capable of differentiating between page layouts, symbols, languages and font styles.
